Send a bot to a meeting

The bot joins the meeting, records, and transcribes it automatically. Works with Google Meet, Zoom, and Microsoft Teams.

Paste the link from Google Meet, Zoom, or Microsoft Teams.

This is the name participants will see when the bot joins.

What happens next

  1. The bot will join your meeting within ~30 seconds.
  2. Meeting platforms may show a "Someone wants to join" prompt — admit the bot.
  3. The bot records audio while it's in the meeting.
  4. When the meeting ends, audio is downloaded and transcribed.
  5. The transcript appears under All recordings when ready.
