Tips

  • Files up to 500 MB are supported. Large meeting recordings (1+ hour) work fine.
  • Transcription runs in the background — you'll be redirected to the transcript page where it auto-refreshes.
  • Whisper transcribes at roughly 2× realtime on CPU. A 30-minute file takes ~10-15 minutes.
